Our History

Where The Drink's & Food’s As Good As The Rooftop Views.

Wings 23 is a rooftop restaurant that serves delicious, Afro-continental food in a fun, relaxed dining environment. It’s a perfect spot for friends and family alike to eat and drink as if they are in their own home.

Centered around an Afro-Continental, we create sophisticated yet familiar dishes using expert cooking techniques. With a frequently updated menu of Wings 23-fired tastes, housemade pastas, and farm fresh salads, Wings 23 is a celebration of rustic cuisine that is thoughtful of the season.
